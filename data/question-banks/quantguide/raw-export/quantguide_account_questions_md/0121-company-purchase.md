### 题干

How much would you pay for a company that generates $\$100$ of cash flow every single year, until you sell, if you have a targeted yield of $16\%$?
$$$$
Enter your solution rounded to the nearest whole number.

### Hint

To calculate how much you would pay, you simply divide your cash flows by your targeted yield.


### 解答

To calculate how much you would pay, you simply divide your cash flows by your targeted yield.
$$$$
So $\frac{\$100}{.16} = \$625$
