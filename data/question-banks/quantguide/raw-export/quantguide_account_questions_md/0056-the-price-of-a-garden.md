### 题干

A neighbor told you he was offered a triangular piece of ground for a garden. Its sides were $55$ yards, $62$ yards, and $117$ yards. The price was $\$10$ per square yard. What will its cost be?

### Hint

Use the triangle inequality. 

### 解答

From the triangle inequality, we see that $55 + 62 =117$. This violates the triangle inequality, which says that any two sides must sum to a number strictly larger than the third side. This is impossible and the triangle will have $0$ area, so the answer is $0$.
