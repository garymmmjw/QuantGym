### 题干

Find the angle (in degrees) between the minutes and hours hands at $9:45$ PM on a standard clock.

### Hint

At 9:45 PM, where is the minutes hand located on the clock? How long into an hour is 45 minutes?

### 解答

At 9:45 PM, we know the minutes hand is at the $9$. The hours hand is $\dfrac{3}{4}$ of the way between the $9$ and the $10$, as $\dfrac{3}{4}$ of an hour has elapsed. Therefore, the angle between them is just $\dfrac{3}{4}$ of the angular distance from $9$ to $10$ on the clock. Namely, as there are $12$ equally spaced numerical values and the total degrees of a circle is $360$, there are $30$ degrees between each numerical value. Therefore, our answer is $\dfrac{3}{4} \cdot 30 = 22.5$.
