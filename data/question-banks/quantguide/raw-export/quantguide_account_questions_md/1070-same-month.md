### 题干

What is the minimum amount of people needed in a group to guarantee at least two of them have a birthday in the same month?

### Hint

There are $12$ months in a year.

### 解答

Given that there are $12$ months, if we have $13$ people, by the Pigeonhole Principle, two must share a birthday in the same month.
