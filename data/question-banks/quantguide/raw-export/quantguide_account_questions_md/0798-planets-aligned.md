### 题干

There is a solar system with three planets orbiting around the sun. One of them has a translation period of $40$ years, another one of $60$ years and another one of $90$ years. Today, the three planets are aligned with the sun. When is the next time, in years, the three planets will be aligned with the sun?

### Hint

The planets can be aligned either on the same or opposite side of the sun. Cut all the periods in half.

### 解答

The planets can be aligned either on the same or opposite side of the sun. Therefore, the periods are really $20,30,$ and $45$. We want the least common multiple of these, which is simply $180$.
