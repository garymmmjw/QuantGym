### 题干

Find the integer $x$ such that $x^3 = 441^3 + 588^3 + 735^3$.

### Hint

Write $441 = 147 \cdot 3, 588 = 147 \cdot 4,$ and $735 = 147\cdot 5$

### 解答

We can write $441 = 147 \cdot 3, 588 = 147 \cdot 4,$ and $735 = 147\cdot 5$, so $441^3 + 588^3 + 735^3 = 147^3(3^3 + 4^3 + 5^3) = 147^3 \cdot 216 = (6 \cdot 147)^3 = 882^3$. Therefore, $x = 882$.
