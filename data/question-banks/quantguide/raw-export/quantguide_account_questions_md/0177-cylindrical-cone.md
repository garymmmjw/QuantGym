### 题干

Suppose that a point $(X,Y,Z)$ is uniformly randomly selected from the upwards-facing cylinder of radius $r$ and height $h$ whose lower base is centered at $(0,0,0)$. Find the probability that this point also lies in the cone whose base and height is the same as the cylinder.

### Hint

The volume of the cone that is inside this cylinder is $\dfrac{1}{3}\pi r^2h$

### 解答

The volume of the cylinder is $\pi r^2 h$. The volume of the cone that is inside this cylinder is $\dfrac{1}{3}\pi r^2h$. Therefore, since we select uniformly at random from the cylinder, the probability it lies in the cone is $\dfrac{\frac{1}{3}\pi r^2h}{\pi r^2h} = \dfrac{1}{3}$
