### 题干

$$X, Y, Z \overset{\text{iid}}{\sim}\text{Unif}(0, 1)$. What is the probability that $X \geq Y \geq Z$?

### Hint

By symmetry, an ordering of $X, Y, Z$ is equally likely to occur as any other ordering.

### 解答

By symmetry, an ordering of $X, Y, Z$ is equally likely to occur as any other ordering. There are a total of $6$ orderings, only one of which satisfying $X \geq Y \geq Z$, so our answer is $\frac{1}{6}$.
