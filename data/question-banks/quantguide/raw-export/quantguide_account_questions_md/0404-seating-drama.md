### 题干

Five people, denoted $1,2,3,4,5$, sit around a circular table of $5$ seats. $1$ does not want to sit next to either one of $2$ or $5$. Additionally, $4$ wishes to sit immediately to the right of $2$ while $2$ sits immediately to the right of $5$. Who is to the left of $5$?

### Hint

Drawing out the circular table is helpful to visualize this.

### 解答

We know that, from left to right, we have $XY524$, where $X$ and $Y$ are each one of either $1$ or $3$. Since $1$ doesn't want to sit next to $5$, $Y = 3$, so person $3$ is to the left of person $5$.
