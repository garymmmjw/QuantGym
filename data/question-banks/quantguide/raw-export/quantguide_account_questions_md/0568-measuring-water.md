### 题干

The maid was given the task of fetching exactly 2 pints of water from the brook using two vessels, one measuring 7 pints and the other measuring 11 pints. To accomplish this, what is the minimum number of transactions required? A "transaction" can involve filling a vessel, emptying it, or pouring water from one vessel to another.
