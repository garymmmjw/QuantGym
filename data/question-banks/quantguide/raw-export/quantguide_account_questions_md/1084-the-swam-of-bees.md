### 题干

The square root of half the number of bees in a swarm has flown out upon a bush; eight-ninths of the whole swarm has remained behind; Then, one female and one male bee exit the bush to be caught by a bird. How many total bees were there at the start? 

### Hint

Create an equation for the total number of bees. 

### 解答

Let $n$ be the number of bees. We have that:

$$\sqrt{\frac{n}{2}} + \frac{8n}{9} + 2 = n$$

Solving this, we get $n = 72, 9/2$. It is impossible to have a fraction number of bees, so $72$ is our answer.
