### 题干

You roll $10$ fair $6-$sided dice and sum the values. What is the probability that the sum is divisible by $6$?

### Hint

Let the sum of the first $9$ dice be $x$. What are the possible values of the total sum of the 10 dice?

### 解答

Let the sum of the first 9 dice be $x$. Then with equal probability, the sum of all the dice is $x+1,\dots, x+6$. Exactly one of these 6 values is divisible by $6$, so our answer is $\dfrac{1}{6}$.
