### 题干

A 100-pound watermelon is $99\%$ water. After spending some time in the sun, it has only dried down to $98\%$ water. How much does the watermelon weigh now? 

### Hint

There is $1$ pound of non-water in the watermelon. What proportion of the weight is this?

### 解答

There is $1$ pound of non-water in the watermelon. After drying, we know that this pound consists of $2\%$ of the total weight of the watermelon. Hence, the watermelon must weight 50 pounds.
