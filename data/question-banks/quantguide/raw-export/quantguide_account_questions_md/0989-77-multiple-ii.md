### 题干

What is the smallest multiple of $77$ that is at least $700,000$?

### Hint

Consider $770000$ and $77000$ first.

### 解答

Since $770000 = 77 \cdot 10000$ and $77000 = 77 \cdot 1000$ are multiples of $77$, $693000 = 770000 - 77000$ is also a multiple of $77$. Since $7700 = 77 \cdot 100$ is a multiple of $77$, $693000 + 7700 = 700700$ is a multiple of $77$. Lastly, since $693$ is the largest multiple of $77$ that is at most $700$, $700700 - 693 = 700007$ is the smallest multiple of $77$ at least $700000$.
