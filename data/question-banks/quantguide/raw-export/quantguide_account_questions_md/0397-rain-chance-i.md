### 题干

This weekend, there is a $40\%$ chance it rains on Saturday and a $70\%$ chance it rains on Sunday. Assuming independence, what is the probability it does not rain this weekend?

### Hint

What's the probability it does not rain each day?

### 解答

The probability it doesn't rain on the two days are $0.6$ and $0.3$, respectively. Therefore, with independence, the probability it doesn't rain on either day is $0.6 \cdot 0.3 = 0.18$.
