### 题干

Yungeun rolls $9$ fair dice simultaneously. Compute the probability that the product of the $9$ values obtained is divisible by $4$.

### Hint

The probability that the product is divisible by 4 is equal to 1 minus the probability that the product is divisible by 2 (case 1) or is odd (case 2).

### 解答

The probability that the product is divisible by 4 is equal to 1 minus the probability that the product is divisible by 2 (case 1) or is odd (case 2). For case 1 to occur, there must be exactly 1 value that is divisible by 2; all others must be odd. This occurs with probability
\[\begin{aligned}
\mathbb{P}(\text{case 1}) &= \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^8 \frac{1}{3} \binom{9}{1}.
\end{aligned}\]
Case 2 occurs with probability
\[\begin{aligned}
\mathbb{P}(\text{case 2}) &= \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^9.
\end{aligned}\]
Our solution is
\[\begin{aligned}
1 - \mathbb{P}(\text{case 1}) - \mathbb{P}(\text{case 2}) &= 1 - \frac{3}{2^8} - \frac{1}{2^9} \\
&= \frac{2^9 - 6 - 1}{2^9} \\
&= \frac{505}{512}. 
\end{aligned}\]
