### 题干

You have to deposit money into your bank account. All of the bills are either $\$10$ or $\$20$. You know that you deposited a total of $150$ bills and the bank teller said you deposited $\$2150$. How many $\$20$ bills did you deposit?

### Hint

This is a classic systems of equations problem. 

### 解答

This is a classic systems of equations problem. Let $A$ be the number of $\$10$ bills you deposited and $B$ be the number of $\$20$ bills you deposited. Since you deposited $150$ bills, you know that $A+B=150$. Another equation you can make is the "value" equation. Since the total value you deposited is $\$2150$, you know that the value from the $\$10$ bills is $10A$ and $20B$ from the $\$20$ bills. Thus $10A+20B=2150$. When we solve these two equations, we get $B = 65$.
