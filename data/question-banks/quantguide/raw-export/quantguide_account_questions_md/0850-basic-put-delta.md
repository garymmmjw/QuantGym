### 题干

What is the $\Delta$ of a deep in-the-money put?

### Hint

What does the payoff of a put look like at expiry? 

### 解答

If a put is deep in-the-money, it essentially acts like the underlying itself. A put is essentially another way to short (or sell) a stock. Selling a stock has a delta of $-1$ (think about taking the derivative of the stock with respect to itself). So, a deep ITM put will also have a delta of $-1$.
