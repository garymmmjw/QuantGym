### 题干

A father leaves a $\$100$ inheritance to his two sons, Abe and Ben. If one-third of Abe's inheritance is to be taken from one-fourth of Ben's inheritance, the difference would be $\$11$. How much did Abe receive?

### Hint

Let $x$ be the amount Abe receives. Then $100-x$ is what Ben receives. Write the line in the question algebraically in terms of $x$.

### 解答

Let $x$ be the amount Abe receives. Then $100-x$ is what Ben receives. The line there says that $\dfrac{100-x}{4} - \dfrac{x}{3} = 11$. Solving for $x$ here algebraically yields $x = 24$.
