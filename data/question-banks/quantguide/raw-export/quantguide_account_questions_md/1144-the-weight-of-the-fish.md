### 题干

A man caught a fish. The tailed weighed $9$ ounces. The head weighed as much as the tail and half the body, and the body weighed as much as the head and tail together. What is the weight of the fish? Give the answer in ounces. 



### Hint

Setup a system of equations 

### 解答

Let $t$, $h$, and $b$ denote the weight in ounces of the tail, head, and body respectively. 

We have the following $3$ equations:

$$\begin{align*}
&t = 9 \\ 
&h = t + \frac{b}{2} \\ 
&b = h + t 
\end{align*}$$

Solving this system of equations, we obtain $h = 27$, $b = 36$. So $h + b + t = 72$.
