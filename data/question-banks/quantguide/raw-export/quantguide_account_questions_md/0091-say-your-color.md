### 题干

If WHITE=000, RED-101, BLUE-110, and PURPLE-100, then what three-digit string corresponds to YELLOW?

### Hint

Think about consonants versus vowels.

### 解答

The pattern here is that for the first three sounds, $0$ means that the vowel is pronounced and $1$ means that the consonant is pronounced. This means that YELLOW is $001$.
