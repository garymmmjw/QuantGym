### 题干

Is 1027 a prime number (1 for yes and 0 for no)?

### Hint

A number is considered composite if it has more than two factors. Can 1027 be factored?

### 解答

No, 1027 is not prime because it is factorable.

$$1027 = 1000 + 27 = 10^3 + 3^3 = (10+3)(10² + 3² - 10 \times 3) = 13 \times 79$$
