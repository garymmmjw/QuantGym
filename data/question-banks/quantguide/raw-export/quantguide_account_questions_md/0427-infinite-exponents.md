### 题干

If $x^{x^{...}} = 2$, what is $x$ (rounded to the nearest whole number)?

### Hint

There are an infinite number of powers of x, so what happens when we bring all of these to the power of x?

### 解答

Let $y = x^{x^{...}} = 2$. Then, $$x^y = x^{x^{...}} \newline x^2 = 2 \newline x = \sqrt{2}$$
