### 题干

The correlation between $X$ and $Y$ is 0.56. What is the correlation between $8X$ and $Y+7$?

### Hint

What is the definition of correlation and does adding or multiply constants to the random variables change either the numerator or denominator?

### 解答

The correlation between $X$ and $Y$ is defined as the ratio of their covariance to the product of their standard deviations, none of which are affected by multiplication or addition of constants. The correlation is thus still $0.56$.
