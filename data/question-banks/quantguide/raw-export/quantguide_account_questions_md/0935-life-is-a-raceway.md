### 题干

Lightning McQueen goes for a race. He places $14$ places ahead of last place and $6$ places below the upper half of competitors. What place was Lightning McQueen in?

### Hint

Let $x$ be Lightning McQueen's place. We know the upper half is cutoff is $x-6$, as it is $6$ places better than him. Do similar for last place.

### 解答

Let $x$ be Lightning McQueen's place. We know the upper half is cutoff is $x-6$, as it is $6$ places better than him. We also know that the lowest place is $x+14$, as it is $14$ places worse than him. Therefore, we know that the upper half cutoff is half of the lowest place, so $x-6= \dfrac{x+14}{2}$, meaning that $x = 26$.
