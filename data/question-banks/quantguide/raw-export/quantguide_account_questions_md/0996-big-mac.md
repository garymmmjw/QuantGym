### 题干

In the year $2000$, Big Macs sold for $89$ cents each. Let $x$ be the number of Big Macs you can buy with a $\$20$ bill and $y$ be the change (in cents) you have left over. Find $xy$. Ignore tax or other expenses.

### Hint

Convert to cents first and then find a simple multiple that works.

### 解答

Converting to cents, we know that $89 \cdot 20 = (89 \cdot 2) \cdot 10 = 1780$, so $1780 + 89 \cdot 2 = 1958 = 22 \cdot 89$ is the largest amount you can spend without going on $\$20$. Thus, $x = 22$ and $y = 2000 - 1958 = 42$, so $xy = 22 \cdot 42 = (32 + 10)(32 - 10) = 32^2 - 10^2 = 924$.
