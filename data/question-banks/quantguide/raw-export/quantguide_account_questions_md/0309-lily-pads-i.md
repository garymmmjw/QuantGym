### 题干

A single lily pad sits in an empty pond. Everyday, the lily pad doubles in area until the whole pond is covered- it is known that it would take 10 days for the single lily pad to cover the entire pond. Imagine instead starting with 8 lily pads on the first day. How many days will it take for the surface of the pond to be covered?

### Hint

Each lily pad grows at the game rate, so the 8 lily pads can be thought of as a single lily pad that is three days old. 

### 解答

The 8 lily pads can be thought of as a single lily pad that is three days old, since $2^3=8$. Thus, it will take $10-3=7$ days for the surface of the pond to be covered.
