### 题干

$$5$ fair coins are flipped. Given that you obtained $3$ heads, find the probability that you obtained the sequence $THHTH$.

### Hint

How many sequences of length $5$ are there with three heads?

### 解答

There are $\displaystyle \binom{5}{3} = 10$ sequences of length $5$ containing exactly three heads. Therefore, the probability of this particular sequence is $\dfrac{1}{10}$, as all are equally likely.
