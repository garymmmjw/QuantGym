### 题干

Let $X \sim N(2,4)$ and $Y \sim N(1,4)$. Furthermore, let $Z = 3X - 2Y$. What is the mean of $Z$?

### Hint

The expected value of a constant is a constant. Thus, $E[aX+bY] = aE[X] + bE[Y]$ where $a$ and $b$ are constants. How can this be useful for finding $E[Y]$?

### 解答

$$$E[Z] = E[3X- 2Y] = 3E[X] - 2E[Y] = 6-2=4$$
