### 题干

Let $1, 2, \ldots, 7$ be the days of the week (Sunday, Monday, ..., Saturday). What day of the week will it be 100 days after Monday (as an integer)?

### Hint

How can you use the modulo operator to determine how many days are remaining after the last Monday?

### 解答

Every seventh day is a Monday. There will be $mod(100,7)=2$ days remaining after the last Monday, and thus 100 days after Monday is Wednesday (4).
