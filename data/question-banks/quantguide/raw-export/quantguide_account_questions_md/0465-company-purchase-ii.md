### 题干

QuantGuide Inc. generates $\$100$ of cash flow per year, and its cash flow is expected to grow at $5\%$ per year until you sell. If you invested in another similar company, you could earn $10\%$ per year. How much would you pay to buy QuantGuide Inc.? 

### Hint

$$\text{Company Value} = \frac{\text{Cash Flow}}{\text{Discount Rate - Cash Flow Growth Rate}}$ where $\text{Cash Flow Growth Rate < Discount Rate}$.

### 解答

To calculate, we use the following equation: $$$$
$\text{Company Value} = \frac{\text{Cash Flow}}{\text{Discount Rate - Cash Flow Growth Rate}}$ where $\text{Cash Flow Growth Rate < Discount Rate}$.
$$$$
So, ${\text{Company Value}} = \frac{100}{10\% - 5\%}$ = $\frac{100}{.05}$ = $\$2000$
