### 题干

Given that $\text{Var}(X) = 25$ and $\mathbb{E}[X] = 3$, find $\mathbb{E}[X^2]$.

### Hint

Use the relation $\text{Var}(X) = \mathbb{E}[X^2] - (\mathbb{E}[X])^2$

### 解答

Using the relation $\text{Var}(X) = \mathbb{E}[X^2] - (\mathbb{E}[X])^2$, we have $25 = \mathbb{E}[X^2] - 3^2$, so $\mathbb{E}[X^2] = 34$.
