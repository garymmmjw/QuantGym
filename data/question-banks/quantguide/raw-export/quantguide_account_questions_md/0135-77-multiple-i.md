### 题干

What is the smallest multiple of $77$ that is at least $70,000$?

### Hint

Consider $77000$ and $7700$ first.

### 解答

Since $77000 \cdot 77 \cdot 1000$ and $7700 = 77 \cdot 100$ are multiples of $77$, $69300 = 77000 - 7700$ is as well. We also know that $770 = 77 \cdot 10$ is a multiple of $77$, so $69300 + 770 = 70070$ is also a multiple of $77$. This is the smallest since if we subtract $77$, we are below $70000$.
