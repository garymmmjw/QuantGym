### 题干

You are dealt two cards from a fair deck of 52 cards. What is the probability that you have a pair of aces?

### Hint

What is the probability that the first card you receive is an ace? What is the probability that the second card you receive is an ace given you just received an ace?

### 解答

$$$P(\textrm{two aces}) = P(\textrm{first ace}) \times P(\textrm{second ace} \vert \textrm{first ace})= \frac{4}{52}\times\frac{3}{51}=\frac{1}{221}$$
