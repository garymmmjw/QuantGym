### 题干

Let $X \sim N(2,4)$ and $Y \sim N(1,4)$ be independent. Furthermore, let $Z = 3X - 2Y$. What is the variance of $Z$?

### Hint

Remember that $V[aX+bY] = a² V[X] + b² V[Y]$, where $a$ and $b$ are constants. How can this be useful for solving for $V[Y]$?

### 解答

$$$V[Z] = V[3X-2Y] = 9V[X] + 4V[Y] = 36+16=52$$
