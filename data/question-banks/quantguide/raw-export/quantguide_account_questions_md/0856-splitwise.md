### 题干

You and seven friends go out for lunch. The subtotal is 182.30. You all decide to add a generous 60% tip and split the bill evenly. How much do you pay? If the answer is $\$12.34$, answer $12.34$.

### Hint

Let $X$ be the subtotal. Then, the post-tip total is $1.6X$. What is your split?

### 解答

Let $X$ be the subtotal. Then, the post-tip total is $1.6X$, and your split is $\frac{1.6X}{8} = .2X$. Hence, your split is:

$$2 \times \frac{182.30}{10} = 36.46$$
