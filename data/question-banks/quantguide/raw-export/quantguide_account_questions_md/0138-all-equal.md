### 题干

The median, mean, and mode of 100, 40, 200, 50, 90, 60, and $x$ are all equal to $x$. What is the value of $x$?

### Hint

This could be solved brute force, but this is not the intention of the problem. What equations/formulas could be useful in solving for $x$?

### 解答

We are given that $x$ is the mean. Thus,

$$x = \frac{100 + 40 + 200 + 50 + 90 + 60 + x}{7} \Rightarrow x = 90$$
