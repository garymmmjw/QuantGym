### 题干

You use OLS regression model the relationship between news sentiment, $X$, and AAPL share price, $Y$, and find that $Y = 156.3 + 10.21X$ with a correlation coefficient of $0.78$. You duplicate the data and re-run the OLS regression. How much did the correlation coefficient, $\rho$, increase by?

### Hint

Write out the formula for correlation and understand the effects of sample size on correlation.

### 解答

Correlation is not a function of sample size, and thus correlation increases by 0.
