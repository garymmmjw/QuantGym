### 题干

What is the sum of the integers from 1 to 50, inclusive?

### Hint

There are several solutions to this problem, algebraic and geometric- the Gaussian sum is the most practical approach.

### 解答

There are several solutions to this problem, algebraic and geometric. The most simple is to use the Gaussian sum which can be derived in 2D geometry. The sum of the integers from 1 to $n$ is $\frac{n\times (n+1)}{2}$. In this case, the sum is 1275.
