### 题干

You are at a pond. One day, you decide to catch $50$ fish and tag them. The next day, you capture $40$ fish and $10$ of them have tags. What is your best guess at the number of fish in the pond?

### Hint

$$\dfrac{1}{4}$ of the captured fish were tagged.

### 解答

$$\dfrac{1}{4}$ of the captured fish were tagged. Therefore, this implies that you tagged roughly $\dfrac{1}{4}$ of the fish population in the pond. This means there are roughly $50 \cdot 4 = 200$ fish in the pond.
