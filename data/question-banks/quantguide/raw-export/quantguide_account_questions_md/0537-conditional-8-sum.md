### 题干

Two fair $6-$sided dice were rolled and the sum of the upfaces is $8$. Find the probability that one of the dice showed $6$.

### Hint

List out all the ways to obtain a sum of $8$. How many have a $6$?

### 解答

There are $5$ outcomes of the dice that show a sum of $8$. These are $(6,2), (5,3), (4,4), (3,5),$ and $(2,6)$. Of those, two of them are $(6,2)$ and $(2,6)$. Therefore, our answer is simply $\dfrac{2}{5}$.
