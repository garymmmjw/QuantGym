### 题干

Alice and Bob now are in NASCAR. They continually race around a circular track. Alice can drive a lap in $6$ minutes, while Bob takes $9$ minutes to drive a lap. After how many minutes will Alice lap Bob i.e. catch back up to Bob a lap ahead of him?

### Hint

We can think about this in terms of number of laps completed per Alice lap. For every lap Alice completes, Bob completes $2/3$ of a lap. 

### 解答

We can think about this in terms of number of laps completed per Alice lap. For every lap Alice completes, Bob completes $2/3$ of a lap. This is since it takes Bob $3/2$ as long to complete a lab. Therefore, we note that at the moment Alice completes $3$ laps, Bob completes his second lap, so Alice will be exactly $1$ lap ahead of Bob at this instant. Alice completes $3$ laps after $18$ minutes, so the answer is $18$.
