### 题干

There is a fair 18-sided die where each side is either a 6 or a 3. The expected value of a roll is 4. How many 6s are on the 18-sided die?

### Hint

If there are $n$ sides that are $6$, what is the expected value of the die?

### 解答

Suppose there are $n$ sides labeled 6, and $18 - n$ sides labeled 3. Then, the expected value of a roll can be written as $\frac{6n}{18} + \frac{3(18-n)}{18} = 4$. Solving for $n$, we find
\[\begin{aligned}
    \frac{6n}{18} + \frac{3(18-n)}{18} = 4 \\
    \frac{n}{6} + 3 &= 4 \\
    n &= 6
\end{aligned}\]
