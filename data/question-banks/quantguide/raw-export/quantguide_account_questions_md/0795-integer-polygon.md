### 题干

How many values of $n$ are there such that a regular $n-$gon has interior angles that (in degrees) are integer-valued?

### Hint

The prime factorization of $360$ is $360 = 2^3 \cdot 3^2 \cdot 5$.

### 解答

The prime factorization of $360$ is $360 = 2^3 \cdot 3^2 \cdot 5$. Therefore, there are $(3+1)(2+1)(1+1) = 24$ total factors of $360$. However, we need to remove $1$ and $2$ as factors, as those are not polygons. Therefore, there are $24 - 2 = 22$ values of $n$ that are valid.
