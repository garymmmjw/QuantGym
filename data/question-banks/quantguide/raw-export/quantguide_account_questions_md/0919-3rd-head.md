### 题干

Find the expected number of flips needed to observe $3$ heads on a coin with probability $\dfrac{1}{4}$ of heads per flip.

### Hint

The distribution of flips needed is $N \sim \text{NegBinom}(3,1/4)$

### 解答

The distribution of flips needed is $N \sim \text{NegBinom}(3,1/4)$, which has mean $\mathbb{E}[N] = \dfrac{3}{1/4} = 12$.
