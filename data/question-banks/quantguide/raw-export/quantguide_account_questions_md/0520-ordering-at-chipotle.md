### 题干

There are 10 vegetarian toppings at Chipotle to choose from. How many distinct vegetarian bowl combinations can you make from these toppings (a bowl with no toppings does not count)?

### Hint

For each topping, you have the binary option to either get it or not.

### 解答

For each topping, you have the binary option to either get it or not. Thus, there are $2^{10} - 1$ different bowl combinations ($-1$ for excluding the null set).
