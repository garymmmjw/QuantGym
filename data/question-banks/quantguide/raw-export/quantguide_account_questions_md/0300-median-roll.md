### 题干

Mike rolls three fair 6-sided dice at once. What is the expected median value of the dice?

### Hint

How can you use symmetry to relate the probability of each value being the median?

### 解答

By the symmetry of the dice, the median value being $3$ and $4$ are equally likely. Similarly, $2$ and $5$ are equally likely, as are $1$ and $6$. As the mean value of each median pair is $\dfrac{7}{2}$, this implies that the median is also $\dfrac{7}{2}$.
